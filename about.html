About Us

Redefining energy solutions through innovation, sustainability, and community impact.

Company Overview

Axflo Oil & Gas Limited is fully registered and licensed to operate in Nigeria. It is driven by goal-oriented policies in which performance is the primary mover. Customer satisfaction is the single most important aspect of any business upon which profits and long-term growth depend.

In association with our working partners, we constantly introduce innovative products, techniques, and technologies that constitute the core of our future growth. Our product line is focused on solutions to the problems in oil production, meeting market demand as well as customer expectations with superior support services.

Our Mission

To deliver cutting-edge energy solutions that empower industries and communities while prioritizing environmental sustainability and technological advancement. Our team of highly trained engineers utilizes a multidiscipline integrated systems approach to solve the most challenging problems in the industry under aggressive time constraints—without sacrificing safety, quality, or performance.

Expertise & Crisis Management

We provide unparalleled expertise, available 24/7. In a crisis or emergency, Axflo’s Spill Response Team can provide immediate assistance anywhere in Nigeria. Our professionals are frequently called upon to handle advanced engineering tasks and emergencies, ensuring optimal performance and client satisfaction.

Our Services

We offer a variety of solutions to support the oil and gas industry:

  • Environmental Services (EIA, Soil and Groundwater Assessment & Remediation)
  • Waste Management (Cuttings Re-injection, Solids Control, Treatment)
  • Petroleum/Hazardous Materials (Hazmat) Storage Tank Management
  • Equipment Leasing, Sales, and Services
  • Supply of Oil Drilling Fluids, Muds, Dispersants, and Other Chemicals
  • Foreign Procurements & Electrical/Instrumentation Services
  • Rotating Equipment & Machinery Reliability Assessment
