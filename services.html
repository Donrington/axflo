Our Services

Empowering Industries with Advanced Solutions

Axflo Oil & Gas offers a range of innovative services that prioritize safety, sustainability, and operational excellence.

Oil Spill Equipment

Axflo Oil & Gas provides cutting-edge oil spill response equipment tailored for rapid deployment in diverse marine, coastal, and onshore environments. Our inventory includes containment booms, high-efficiency oil skimmers, sorbent pads, oil-water separators, and high-capacity vacuum trucks.

Designed to minimize ecological damage and accelerate recovery efforts, our equipment adheres to ISO 14001:2015 environmental management standards. We ensure reliability by conducting regular inspections, maintenance, and readiness drills, enabling clients to execute effective spill response operations without delays.

Test-Running Hydraulic Power Packs

Hydraulic power packs are a vital component of oil spill response and industrial operations, supplying high-pressure hydraulic energy for containment and recovery equipment. At Axflo, we conduct comprehensive testing and commissioning to ensure peak performance and reliability under extreme operating conditions.

Our testing process involves load simulations, pressure calibration, thermal stress evaluation, and efficiency analysis, guaranteeing seamless integration with various hydraulic skimmers, boom deployment systems, and industrial containment units. By adhering to OEM guidelines and industry best practices, we extend equipment lifespan and prevent system failures during critical response operations.

Spill Response Cover for NAOC Brass Terminal

As part of our commitment to environmental safety, Axflo provides comprehensive spill response services at the NAOC Brass Terminal, a key hub for crude oil barging and transportation. Our services include pre-incident planning, active containment measures, emergency spill response mobilization, and post-spill remediation.

Utilizing state-of-the-art skimming systems, dispersant application units, and oil containment booms, our trained response teams mitigate the risks associated with offshore loading, transfer operations, and pipeline leaks. Our expertise in rapid response minimizes downtime, operational disruptions, and environmental liabilities for our clients.

Mangrove Planting and Restoration

Mangroves serve as natural coastal barriers that protect marine ecosystems from the impact of oil spills and industrial activities. Axflo’s mangrove restoration initiatives focus on habitat rehabilitation, erosion control, and biodiversity enhancement in oil-impacted regions.

Our projects involve scientific soil analysis, hydrocarbon contamination assessment, controlled planting techniques, and long-term ecosystem monitoring. By restoring mangrove forests, we help mitigate carbon emissions, strengthen coastal resilience, and support the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) for climate action and marine ecosystem preservation.

Fence Boom Installation at NAOC Loading Facility

To safeguard oil loading terminals and marine logistics hubs, Axflo specializes in the design, deployment, and maintenance of fence booms— a critical first line of defense against oil spills in high-risk offshore environments.

Our heavy-duty containment barriers are engineered for long-term resilience, effectively preventing the spread of hydrocarbons in docking areas, transfer stations, and loading terminals. We utilize high-strength, UV-resistant, and chemical-resistant materials, ensuring that fence booms withstand harsh weather, tidal currents, and prolonged exposure to oil contaminants.

Spill Containment and Recovery Activities

Axflo is an industry leader in spill containment and oil recovery operations, employing advanced containment strategies and hydrocarbon recovery techniques to mitigate the environmental impact of industrial spills.

Our 24/7 emergency response teams deploy containment booms, sorbents, and high-speed skimmers to isolate and recover oil from marine, estuarine, and inland spill zones. We incorporate real-time monitoring, aerial surveillance, and predictive analytics to optimize spill response efficiency and minimize contamination spread.

In line with ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 standards, Axflo ensures that all containment and recovery operations adhere to global best practices in oil spill management and environmental restoration.
